The Outbox Edu Hearing impairment corhort 1

The corhort was launched to improve on service delivery to the persons with visual and hearing impairment.

Below are the agreed signs of the HTML tags and basic content translated into Ugandan signlanguage

Tag - below is a sign for 'tag'

< html>< /html> - Is just finger spelled then tag

< title>< /title> - sign for title or topic then sign tag.

< head>< /head> - The sign for the head tag is spelling the letter h with both hands then the sign for tag.

< body>< /body> - Demonstrate sign of body then sign tag.

< base> - Sign for base/basics then tag.

< meta> - Finger spell meta then sign tag.

< link>Sign link/join then tag
