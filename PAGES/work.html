My Work

Here is what I do.

Create and design websites

I create and design very responsive and high qaulity websites. Using elements like typography, illustration, photography and layouts, I always has an extremely creative mind that can absorb visual trends and deploy them in fresh and exciting ways.

Solor system Installation and Maintainance

I help the locals install and maintain their solar systems. I do this by fixing the solor panels on the roofs in addition to wiring the house and installation ofthe circuts and other related necessary items.

NB: I do this out of my personal experience. I hope to go for a diploma in electrical engineering in the coming years.

Relationship guidence and counselling

I also have experience in relationship counselling and guidence. With this experience, I have been able to help my friends and other people with relationship issues to get on their feet again. I 'facilitate' positive change from what is happening to them at any given time. I am there to help them communicate in a way in which they can learn to listen and talk to each other. Many people shout or talk at each other and may have to learn to listen and talk to each other.

Voluntary Ugandan Sign language training

Since I have a hearing imperment, I ventured into learning sign language with the aim of supplementing and making my communication easier.

Now that am good at sign language, i decided to voluntarily train people who fall in the same category as me and their parents as well as those who wish to learn sign language.
